How does Babee personalize content?
Babee uses information about your baby's age, feeding type, sleep patterns, and your specific concerns to generate personalized tips and advice using AI technology.
What's included in the free version?
Free users get daily welcome messages, 3 daily tips, 2 AI chat messages per day, and 1 daily content refresh.
What do I get with Premium?
Premium includes unlimited AI chat (voice & video), unlimited tip refreshes, smart notifications (7 categories), multiple baby profiles, and priority support.
How do Smart Notifications work?
Smart Notifications are AI-generated messages sent at different times throughout the day. They include morning motivation, activity suggestions, feeding tips, sleep advice, and more. You'll receive 2-4 notifications daily when enabled.
Can I track multiple babies?
Yes! Premium members can create unlimited baby profiles and easily switch between them to get personalized content for each child.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. All your data is encrypted and stored securely. We're GDPR compliant and never share your information with third parties. You can delete your account anytime.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through your App Store Account Settings. Cancel at least 24 hours before renewal to avoid being charged.
Which languages does Babee support?
Babee supports 23 languages including English, Turkish, German, French, Spanish, Portuguese, Russian, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Hindi, Arabic, and many more.
How do I restore my Premium purchase?
Go to Profile ‚Üí Premium ‚Üí "Restore Purchases" button. This will restore your subscription if you reinstalled the app or switched devices.

  • Try "Restore Purchases" in the Premium section
  • Contact us if the issue persists
